Meet the Team

Joohi Chauhan, PhD

Principal Investigator & Lab Head

Dr Joohi is currently a faculty in MNNIT Allahabad and has considerable experience in the field of computer vision, Deep Learning and application of machine learning technologies in the field of medical research. She has previously worked on skin burns ( in collaboration with Safdarjung burns unit) during her PhD at IIT Ropar and digital pathology problems during her postdoctorate at University of California. She has also previously been a visiting researcher at NUS Singapore and Cardiff University, UK.

Prateek Mittal

Research Assistant & Lab Coordinator

Prateek is B.E Computer Science graduate from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology. He has worked as a remote research intern at IIT Ropar and a Research Associate (Remote) at Van dijk Lab at Yale University, USA. He is currently working full time as a Graduate Engineer Trainee (IT- Lab Ops) at Dr Lal PathLabs. Prateek manages the research projects and the lab operations. He actively collaborates on projects involving predictive modeling, data-driven insights and applications of deep learning and machine learning technologies to healthcare problems. He is known for his organizational skills and technical expertise in statistical modeling and Python programming. He also loves teaching and has been doing the same in various capacities over the past few years.
